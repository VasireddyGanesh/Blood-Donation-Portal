Donation Portal is a web application where people who are in need of blood can get the information about blood donors. This portal provides a platform where the blood donors and blood receivers available at a single platform by their registrations according to donor and receiver roles. Whenever someone is in need of blood ,they can visit blood donation portal and can search for the required blood group then respective blood donor’s information will be shown to the blood recipient . The main aim of this project is to “SAVE LIVES” through blood donations.
